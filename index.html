I am a MS/PhD student in University of Massachusetts Amherst. Currently, I’m working in The Secure, Private Internet Research Group (SPIN) under professor Amir Houmansadr.

Research Interests

My research interests are focused on computer networks and distributed systems using deep learning approach. Recently, offering real-time streaming to the user is my new interest. In more detail, I’m interested in:

  • AI-driven Network
  • Computer Networks
  • Distributed Systems
  • Edge Computing
  • Federated Learning
  • Deep-learning
  • Real-time Video Streaming
  • Network Security
  • Internet of Things
